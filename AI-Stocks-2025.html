How to Build Your AI Stock Portfolio

Having a list of good AI stocks is great, but knowing how to invest in them is crucial. Here's a practical framework:

Step 1: Understand Your Risk Tolerance

Are you comfortable with the stock dropping 20% in a bad month? If not, stick with established companies like Microsoft.

Step 2: Diversify Your Investments

  • 50% to established, large-cap AI companies (like Microsoft or NVIDIA)

  • 20% to newer or more speculative AI plays

Step 3: Think Long-Term

AI stocks can bounce around in the short term. But if you're betting on the long-term transformation of industries by AI, short-term dips are opportunities to buy more.
